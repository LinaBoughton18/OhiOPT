What is OPT?

OPT (Optional Practical Training) is a temporary employment for international students with an F-1 visa to stay and work in the U.S. after they graduate. Essentially, OPT lets students get a job related to their area of study without reapplying for student visa.

Once you have OPT status, you are authorized to work in the U.S. at companies that support OPT for a certain length of time (usually 12 months).
